#### **THE FIREWALL MINDSET HAVEN**

##### **( A Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist )**





##### *✅DO'S*



* ###### Think before you post; make sure that your content cannot be misinterpreted, misused or manipulated in any way.
* ###### Toggle and enable two-factor authentication on your social media apps.
* ###### Use robust and unique passwords to protect your accounts.
* ###### Verify the identity of the people before you accept friend or follow requests.
* ###### Exercise caution while clicking on the link or downloading the file to prevent phishing or malware attacks.

###### 



##### ❌*DONT'S*



* ###### Don’t send private photos or videos to anyone, these can be used for blackmail by individuals.
* ###### Sharing personal details like your address, phone number or live location.
* ###### Falling for fake giveaways, job offers or requests for money/OTPs.
* ###### Engaging with threatening or abusive messages; block and report them instead.
* ###### Never trust strangers who quickly seek personal information or build emotional connections.
