#### **THE DIGITAL RINSE**

##### **(A "Stay Safe Online" checklist)** 





##### *✅ PREVENTIONS*



* ###### Take a pause before acting; scammers want to catch you in a state of panic, curiosity, or enthusiasm.
* ###### Adopt a "nil trust default" approach to the internet; just because you know someone doesn't mean they're not a scammer or hacker.
* ###### Keep your financial apps logged out rather than trusting background security.
* ###### Don't sync sensitive information like OTP SMS or passwords across multiple devices or browsers.
* ###### Rename your important contacts to avoid confusion with imposter contacts.
* ###### Verify any requests for money or assistance through a different channel.
* ###### Clear all sessions on a shared or borrowed device as any access in the past few minutes can compromise your account.
* ###### Save evidence, not emotions; document your experience calmly before acting or confronting.
* ###### **UPI Safety**: Be wary of small transactions from unknown sources; they might be test transactions to catch you.
* ###### **UPI Safety**: "Scan to receive money" is a red flag; money transfer needs scanning, not receiving
